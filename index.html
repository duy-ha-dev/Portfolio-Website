Recent Projects

Portable IV Stand

Team IV League (Fall 2020 Semester)

Along with three other student teammates and a design mentor, we developed an intuitive and portable IV stand, strictly following the Engineering Design Process. Our client was the Barretos Cancer Hospital, Brazil's most advanced oncological hospital. The hospital needs cost-efficient and portable IV stands to treat at-home patients. IV League's final design utilizes durable and cheap materials such as HDPE and aluminium. Along with functioning like a hospital-grade IV stand, it weighs 3.2kg and fits within 1.82 cubic foot when compressed.
